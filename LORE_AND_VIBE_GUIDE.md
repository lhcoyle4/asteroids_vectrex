# FULIGIN: Lore & Vibe Guide

## The Core Aesthetic: Neon-Decay & Retro-Futurism
*Fuligin* marries the baroque, dying-universe atmosphere of **Gene Wolfe's Book of the New Sun** and **Jack Vance's Dying Earth** with the surreal, mutated landscapes of **Caves of Qud**. We filter this ancient, post-historic sci-fantasy through a minimalist, high-contrast **retro arcade** lens. 

Think glowing neon lasers slicing through the cold, dead ashes of a dying red sun.

### Visual Pillars
- **Palette**: Pitch-black (fuligin) backgrounds contrasted with vibrant, toxic neon (cyan, magenta, acid green) and the deep crimson of a dying star (cinnabar).
- **UI & Typography**: Minimalist, sleek, and clinical. Stylized, jagged, retro-future fonts.
- **Graphics**: Chroma/Glassmorphism combined with classic Vector Line Art. For example, a wireframe spaceship silhouette in glowing cyan against a pure black "Urthgrid".
- **Contrast**: The juxtaposition of ancient, holy relics and archaic terminology with fast-paced, momentum-based arcade loops.

## The World & Lore
Set in the **Latter-Day**—the final age of a dying cosmos. The sun is a swollen red cinder ("Solar Rust"), and the void is littered with the detritus of dead epochs.
- **The Setting**: Orbital graveyards, swirling gyres of debris, and ancient terminus stations.
- **The Inhabitants**: Bizarre off-worlders (*Cacogens*), voiceless antagonists (*Ascians*), and trickster rogues (*Cugels*) taking dangerous gambles in the void.
- **The Objective**: Salvage ancient holy items (*Reliquaries*) and survive the deadly gauntlet of the *Shorn Star*.

## Naming & Terminology (Short, Punchy, Minimalist)
The lexicon blends the archaic and the arcade.

### Single-Word Hits (Mysterious & Clinical)
- **FULIGIN**: The color darker than black. The title itself—a vibrant neon word on a pitch-black screen.
- **CACOGEN**: Sharp, bizarre off-worlders.
- **CUGEL**: The trickster rogue.
- **AUTARCH**: The ultimate high-score objective or final boss.
- **ASCIAN**: Sleek, clinical antagonists.
- **LICTOR**: A sharp, violent, and minimalist rank (executioner).
- **RELIQUARY**: Salvageable ancient holy items.

### "Arcade Grid" & Sci-Fantasy Mashups
- **Urthgrid**: The dying world rendered as a 3D wireframe.
- **Gravegrid**: Neon vector lines mapping orbital graveyards.
- **Decaywave**: Synthwave meets dying universe.
- **Gyre-Vect / Vect-Urth**: The swirling debris, styled as 1982 vector graphics.
- **Nocturne Vect**: The eternal night of a dying cosmos in vector lines.

### High-Contrast Neon-Decay
- **Neon Embers**: Glowing lasers through dead ashes.
- **Cinnablast**: The red-mercury color of the dying sun meets arcade action.
- **Solar Rust / Photic Rust**: The very light of the stars is rusting.
- **Chondrite Glow**: Asteroids radiating with neon energy or shield impacts.
- **Red Sun Drive**: High momentum under a dying star.

### Picaresque Action Loops (The Roguelite Run)
- **Cugel’s Run**: A dangerous, fast-paced salvage loop.
- **The Claw**: Both a holy relic and the iconic arcade grabber.
- **Void Relic**: The core gameplay loop—chasing ancient tech in the dark.
- **Terminus Run**: A deadly executioner's gauntlet.
