FULIGIN

Lore & Vibe Guide

A minimalist, high-contrast retro arcade synthesis of Gene Wolfe's Book of the New Sun, Jack Vance's Dying Earth, and the surreal landscapes of Caves of Qud.

Set in the Latter-Day, the universe is dying under the Solar Rust of a swollen red cinder. You are a Cugel, taking dangerous gambles in orbital graveyards, salvaging holy Reliquaries while hunted by voiceless Ascian executioners.
